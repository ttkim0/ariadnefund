Hourly forecast samples — diagnostic only

Why this looks lower than the daily HIGH chart above: The hourly model is sampled at exactly 7 horizons from issuance time: +1h, +3h, +6h, +12h, +24h, +48h, +72h. None of those land at SF's typical daily peak hour (12-13 PST), so the points below systematically miss the warmest part of each day. For Kalshi daily-HIGH/LOW contracts, use the daily-extreme charts above (which are produced by a different model targeting day-max/day-min directly).
